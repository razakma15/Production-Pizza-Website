The Birth Place Of Modern Pizzerias

The roots of this company lie deep within Naples thoughout the 1940's and 50's where food was scarce due to the civil war that ensued after Mussolini was overthrown. As the country was burning our founder Bernardo Chiellini built the first modern pizzeria in the embers of a dying nation in an effort to unite people and stop the country deteriorating further. Bernardo believed food was the best way to bring people together and that mentality runs though the company veins even to this day.

Traditional Qualities - Contemporary Values

We honor Bernardo's legacy today by upholding his uncompromising quality standards on which he built the company on. This standard is the primary driving force behind our explosive growth in recent years, we now operate in 40 different countries but our headquarters still remains in the original Naples pizzeria.

Looking To The Future

We aim to continue to improve upon the foundations that we were established upon and eventually spread the brand to all corner's of the globe while also maintaining our integrity towards employess and customers. We hope you as the customer join us on this adventure of ascending the boundaries of pizza innovation.
